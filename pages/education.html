Think about it: How did you even get on our website?
Was it because of an email that was sent to you?
Or was it a link that your friend shared with you?
Or was it because of some other method?
Regardless, had you not clicked on one those links, you would not have even been here.
Thus, to prevent yourself from falling into these online scams or phishing, always remember to check the URL of the link before you click on it!
Hover over the link allows you to see its actual URL.
This feature is built-in for almost all modern-day apps.
If you see the URL is different from what you remember, do not click on it!
Copy the URL and search it using Google to make sure it is legitmate!
